My Favorite Horror Films:

  1. Donnie Darko

    • What is real and what is just our imagination?
    • How many influential people do we know that are hiding their grotesque habits and illegal activities?
    • Should we always sacrifice the few to save the many?
    • Who and what is Frank and who does he truly represent? Is he an outwaard image of Donnie Darko himself?




  2. My Suicide
    • Cult Classic
    • Teaches you the importance and meaning of life.
    • Phenomenal camera work and creative design.
    • Brought me back to Earth during my mental health struggles.




  3. The Devil's Advocate

    • My introduction to the world of Physchological Horror
    • A very human and real relationship with Satan and his power in the real world
    • Conveys the age-long battle of women being taken seriously and getting the help, love, and support that they need.
    • The end has you questioning everything that happened throughout the entire film.
